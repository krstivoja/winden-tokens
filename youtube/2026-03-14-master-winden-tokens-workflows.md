BEST PRACTICES

Naming Conventions
• Use clear, descriptive names
• Follow consistent patterns (e.g., color/primary/500)
• Consider semantic naming for better understanding
• Include context in token names

Organization
• Group related tokens together
• Use collections to separate concerns
• Maintain a logical hierarchy
• Keep token sets focused and manageable

Maintenance
• Regular audits of unused tokens
• Update documentation as tokens evolve
• Test changes across your design system
• Communicate updates to your team
