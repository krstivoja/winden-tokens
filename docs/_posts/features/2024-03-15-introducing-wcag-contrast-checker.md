We're excited to announce a major new feature in Winden Tokens: **WCAG Contrast Checker**!

## Why This Matters

Accessibility isn't optional—it's essential. With over 1 billion people worldwide living with disabilities, ensuring your designs meet WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) standards is crucial for creating inclusive experiences.

## What's New

Our new contrast checker helps you:

- ✅ **Real-time validation** - See WCAG compliance instantly as you edit colors
- ✅ **AA & AAA levels** - Support for both WCAG 2.1 AA and AAA standards
- ✅ **Text size aware** - Different requirements for normal and large text
- ✅ **Visual indicators** - Clear pass/fail badges right in your token table

## How It Works

Simply select any two colors in your design tokens, and Winden Tokens will automatically calculate the contrast ratio and show whether it meets WCAG standards for:

- **Normal text** (minimum 4.5:1 for AA, 7:1 for AAA)
- **Large text** (minimum 3:1 for AA, 4.5:1 for AAA)
- **UI components** (minimum 3:1)

## Pro Tips

1. **Test early, test often** - Check contrast ratios as you build your color palette
2. **Document compliant pairs** - Use our notes feature to mark accessible color combinations
3. **Think beyond black and white** - Many color combinations can be accessible with the right tones

Making accessible design easier is just one more way we're helping you create better design systems. Try it out and let us know what you think!
